Projects

Studious was a web based note taking and calendar app geared towards college students. The project took place during my ARCH summer semester in Junior year as a collaborative effort with 4 other students. The idea behind the web app was to allow students to upload their class schedule from an existing tool, QUACCs, and have their classes displayed and ready to take notes. The app offered a uniwue solution where each class, and each session of that class, would hold it's own folder of notes. This would allow students to 'log' notes from each class, allowing for better organization and easier studying. This project was my introduction to web development as well as the development structure SCRUM. I learned a lot about AGILE methodologies and what it takes to work in a small SCRUM team on a weekly deliverable cycle. While I don't believe I will return to the project, I'm incredibly proud of what we managed to achieve in the time frame as a first web app. Everything from designing a logo to launching a real site for our final presentation served to prepare me for future projects and classes.



BLOCKCHAIN BAZAAR

Blockchain Bizaar was a project I ran with two of my classmates in AI and Blockchain during my junior year. The goal of the project was to use an AI model to price unique items, represented by NFTs, based on their stats. The project saw the integration of multiple models for buying and selling the items, a website to interact with the item store, and an Ethereum smart contract to both generate and record ownership of the items. During the project I learned about integration between the web and blockchain as well as several AI models that were tested to find an optimal solution for our use case. I gained import skills in team leadership as well as presenting data and deliverables on a weekly basis. I do hope to one day improve or redo this project without the limitations of the class project both in scope and time. These project version as well as any future versions can be found on my github.
